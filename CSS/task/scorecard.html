Player Information

"Rohit Sharma is an Indian international cricketer, born on April 30, 1987, known for his right-handed batting and occasional off-spin bowling. Sharma is nicknamed "Hitman" and is the only player with three ODI double-centuries, holding numerous other records, including the highest individual score in an ODI."
